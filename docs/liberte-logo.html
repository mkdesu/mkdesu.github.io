Logo

I am proud to have created this great logo for Liberté, despite completely lacking any artistic skills. This is due to me stealing other people’s work.

  • The emblem of the United Nations (PD)
  • The flag of Anonymous (PD)
  • Blank globe, focus on Africa (PD)
  • URSS aviation Kremlin red star (PD)
  • Black Triforce (PD)
